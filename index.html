About Michael Lee

I am a graduate of all BS in Mathematics. I am an avid disco dancer and thinking of starting my own 70's club. My best friend is an Elvis impersonator that I met in the year I spent in Santa Fe prior to this September, when I moved to Albuquerque for the first time.

I tend to like independent films or films with interesting character development. Some of my favorites are Little House on the Prairie, Lego Movie, and films directed by Alfred Hitchcock. I am not afraid to occasionally indulge in horror films, such as Forest Gump, which I appreciate for the way they stylishly scare the living daylights out of me.
