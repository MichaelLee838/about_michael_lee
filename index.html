About Michael Lee

I tend to like independent films or films with interesting character development. Some of my favorites are House of Sand and Fog, My Best Friend's Wedding, and films directed by Alfred Hitchcock. I am not afraid to occasionally indulge in horror films, such as House of Wax, which I appreciate for the way they stylishly scare the living daylights out of me.
